HISCALE Quick Plots

Select a variable and then press <GO> button to see a plot. You can select one or all variables. To select multiple choices, press and hold CTRL key and click left mouse button. To deselect a choice, press and hold CTRL key and click on the selected item. You can also select multiple consequtive items by selecting first item, and then selecting the last item while holding the SHIFT key. You can also browse all figures in here.

Platform: aircraft; Plot type: timeseries
