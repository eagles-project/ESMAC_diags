Introduction

This is a quick plot webpage for Earth System Model Aerosol-Cloud Diagnostic Package (ESMAC Diags), supported by the Enabling Aerosol-cloud interactions at GLobal convection-permitting scalES (EAGLES) project. The version 1 of ESMAC Diags focuses on aerosol evaluation.
 
ESMAC Diags v1 compares E3SM simulated aerosols against in-situ aircraft, ship and surface measurements from field campaigns. Click the field campaign names on the left panel for diagnostic plots. You can also browse the figures here (link).
